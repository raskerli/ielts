## START HERE — 3 STEPS

### Step 1: Open the Tracker App
Open `app/ielts_tracker.html` in your web browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).
The app has:
- Dashboard with progress overview
- Daily task manager
- Mock test score tracker
- Error log
- 40 vocabulary flashcards (+ add your own)
- Speaking practice prompts
- Interactive roadmap

**Important:** Your data is saved in your browser's local storage. Use the same browser and device to keep your data.

### Step 2: Read the Master Roadmap
Open `docs/01_master_roadmap.md` and read the entire 3-month plan. Then come back each week for that week's detailed schedule.

### Step 3: Bookmark Your Resources

## USING THE TRACKER APP

The `ielts_tracker.html` file is a standalone HTML application. No installation required.

Features:
1. **Dashboard** — See all your band scores and weekly progress at a glance
2. **Daily Tasks** — Add and check off your study tasks. Builds a study streak.
3. **Score Tracker** — Log your mock test results and see your improvement chart
4. **Error Log** — Record every mistake with its fix. Never repeat the same error.
5. **Vocabulary Flashcards** — 40 Academic Word List cards + add your own
6. **Speaking Practice** — Random prompts for all 3 Speaking parts with tips
7. **Roadmap** — Interactive view of the full 3-month plan
